Our Journey

A Legacy of Impact

2009

Founded in Chennai

RAISER Energy was established with a team of 5 engineers and a dream to power a greener India.

2013

First 1,000 Installations

Reached a milestone of 1,000 residential solar installations across Tamil Nadu.

2018

Commercial Division Launch

Expanded to serve industrial and commercial clients across South India with MW-scale projects.

2022

National Expansion

Opened offices in 8 cities with 500+ employees and partnerships with global solar manufacturers.

2025

50 MW Milestone

Crossed 50 MW of installed solar capacity — enough to power 40,000 homes.
