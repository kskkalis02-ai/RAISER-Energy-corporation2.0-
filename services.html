Complete Solar Solutions

From design to deployment and beyond — RAISER handles every aspect of your solar journey.

Solar Installation

Our certified engineers design and install solar PV systems tailored to your energy consumption, roof type, and budget. From residential rooftops to large commercial arrays.

  • Site survey and feasibility analysis
  • Custom system design and engineering
  • Grid-connected and off-grid systems
  • Net metering registration support
  • Subsidy and documentation assistance

🔧

Repair & Maintenance

Keep your system performing at peak efficiency. Our maintenance teams provide scheduled servicing, fault diagnostics, and rapid response repairs across all major brands.

  • Annual maintenance contracts (AMC)
  • Panel cleaning and performance checks
  • Inverter diagnostics and replacement
  • Wiring and earthing inspection
  • Emergency response within 24 hours
03
📊

Energy Consulting & Audit

Our energy consultants analyze your power consumption, identify inefficiencies, and recommend the optimal solar solution to maximize ROI. We handle everything from report to subsidy filing.

  • Full energy consumption audit
  • ROI and payback period calculation
  • Government incentive mapping
  • Financial modeling and loan assistance
  • Post-installation performance reporting
  • Smart energy monitoring setup
